The HSK 1 test corresponds to the first level of the hanyu shuiping kaoshi, the official examination of oral and written proficiency in Mandarin Chinese. The HSK 1 vocabulary list is made of 150 chinese words. These HSK 1 words are listed below with their writing in Simplified Chinese, Pinyin (Mandarin), and translation. To learn them quicker, you can also use our HSK 1 Chinese flashcards to practice the simplified, traditional, and pinyin forms, and also access our HSK1 phrases, quizzes and practice test of the same exam level. All of theses resources are available on this page, just check the tabs above to access what you need. You can also purchase our HSK books with the official lists, a must have to get properly prepared, and download in addition the characters of the HSK vocabulary list PDF for free (from the first level to the fourth). Once you complete the level one, you will be able to understand and know how to use very simple words and phrases, meet specific communication needs, and have the ability to learn more. For your information, the oral andreading skills are assessed in two dedicated sections of the test. 120 points out of 200 in total are required to pass the HSK1 test. It lasts 35 minutes and the price (registration fee) is 15 euros. Hanban is the official Chinese entity that administers the exam and the registration is done on its official website. HSK Academy offers a content 100%% dedicated to the exam, offering all the resources to learn and master the Chinese vocabulary required to succeed, through this website, its HSK books, and social networks (links at the bottom of the page).
